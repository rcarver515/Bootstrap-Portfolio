About Me

"I'm like a black unicorn," a joke I've often shared in conversation with many of my Uber client passengers. These days being born and raised in Nashville, a city now full of transplants, has become a rare, borderline fantastic, occurence. Another fun fact about me is that I grew up on a farm. And, I actually got a Tennessee Walking horse (named Sappho) for my 15th birthday, because that's what every teenage black boy wants... Thanks dad.
